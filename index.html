About Hassam

Hassam is a Hong Kong based photographer and graphic designer in Tseung Kwan O, specializing in male-side wedding and event photography, and creative brand visual applications. His work celebrates storytelling through cinematic lighting, emotional direction, and refined aesthetic.

Creative Studio delivers premium projects for discerning clients in Hong Kong, with an emphasis on authenticity, craft, and confidence in every visual asset.

  • Location: Tseung Kwan O, Hong Kong (island-wide availability)
  • Services: Groom wedding events, cultural traditions, portrait cinematography, sports coverage
  • Design: Logos, brochures, thumbnails, branding systems, print & digital assets
  • Style: Dark charcoal, gold accents, minimal layout, premium masculine identity
